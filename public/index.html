About me

Welcome to my website! I'm Philip Carr, a passionate developer located in Central Connecticut. Currently, I'm focused on pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Information Science while actively contributing to the geospatial domain as a data analyst and asset auditor for electric and communication organizations. Over the past two years, I've been fortunate to play a pivotal role in driving innovation and supporting various companies in accomplishing their mapping objectives. As a GIS developer, I offer a distinctive approach to problem-solving and acquiring knowledge in the dynamic world of Geographic Information Systems. If you're interested in exploring more about my journey and expertise, feel free to navigate through the content on this site. I look forward to sharing my experiences and insights with you!
